# 1. 证明 $$\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}\log_{a}{x_{n}}=\log_{a}\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}x_{n}$$
条件： $\text{let }x_{n}>0, \lim\limits_{n\to \infty}x_{n}=1$
证明过程：
$$\begin{align}

\Longrightarrow &\forall \epsilon>0,\exists N,\forall n>N,|x_{n}-1|<\eta \\
\Longrightarrow &1-\eta<x_{n}<1+\eta \\
&\text{为构造 }|\log_{a}{x_{n}}|<\epsilon \\
\Longrightarrow &  a^{-\epsilon}\leqslant 1-\eta<x_{n}<1+\eta\leqslant a^\epsilon  \\
&\text{为了同时满足两个不等号}  \\
\Longrightarrow &\eta = \min\{{a^\epsilon-1,1-a^{-\epsilon}}\}>0 \\
\Longrightarrow &\forall \epsilon>0,\exists n>N,|x_{n}-1|<\eta \\
\Longrightarrow &  a^{-\epsilon}<x_{n}<a^{\epsilon} \\
\Longrightarrow & -\epsilon<\log_{a}{x_{n}}<\epsilon.

\end{align}$$
