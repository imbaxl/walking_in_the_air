连续函数的运算规则

设 $\lim\limits_{x\to x_{0}}f(x)=f(x_{0}),\lim\limits_{x\to x_{0}}g(x)=g(x_{0})$，则
$$\begin{align}
&\lim\limits_{x\to x_{0}}(\alpha f(x)+\beta g(x))=\alpha f(x_{0})+\beta g(x_{0}) \\
&\lim\limits_{x\to x_{0}}(f(x)g(x))=f(x_{0})g(x_{0}) \\
&\lim\limits_{x\to x_{0}}\dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\dfrac{f(x_{0})}{g(x_{0})}\quad (g(x_{0})\neq 0)
\end{align}$$
