# 1. 积化和差公式




$$
\begin{align*} 
&\sin \alpha \cos \beta = \dfrac{\sin(\alpha+\beta)+\sin(\alpha-\beta)}{2} \tag{1} \\
&\cos \alpha \sin \beta = \dfrac{\sin(\alpha+\beta)-\sin(\alpha-\beta)}{2} \tag{2} \\
&\cos \alpha \cos \beta = \dfrac{\cos(\alpha+\beta)+\cos(\alpha-\beta)}{2} \tag{3} \\
&\sin \alpha \sin \beta = -\dfrac{\cos(\alpha+\beta)-\cos(\alpha-\beta)}{2} \tag{4} &

\end{align*}
$$
