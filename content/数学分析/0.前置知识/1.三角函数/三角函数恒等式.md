# 1. 恒等式

## 1.1. 常见恒等式
$$
\begin{align*} 
& \sin^2 x+\cos^2 x=1  \tag{1}\\
\\\
& 1+\cot^2 x = \csc^2 x  \tag{2}\\
& 1+\tan^2 x = \sec^2 x  \tag{3}\\
\\\
& \tan x = \dfrac{\sin x}{\cos x}\tag{4}\\
\\\
& \cot x = \dfrac{\cos x}{\sin x}\tag{5}\\
\\\
& \tan x\cot x=1 \tag{6}

\end{align*}
$$

## 1.2. 其他恒等式

### 1.2.1. 恒等式 $$(1)\cos \dfrac{x}{2}\cos \dfrac{x}{2^2}\cdots \cos \dfrac{x}{2^n}=\dfrac{\sin x}{2^n\sin \dfrac{x}{2^n}}$$
***证明***
1. 由 [[三角函数倍角公式(半角)]] $\sin 2x=2\sin x\cos x$
2. 进一步有 $$\begin{align}
\cos \dfrac{x}{2} \cos \dfrac{x}{4}\cdots\cos \dfrac{x}{2^n}&=\dfrac{\sin x}{2\sin \dfrac{x}{2}}\dfrac{\sin \dfrac{x}{2}}{2\sin \dfrac{x}{4}}\cdots\dfrac{\sin \dfrac{x}{2^{n-1}}}{2\sin \dfrac{x}{2^n}}\\&=\dfrac{\sin x}{2^n\sin \dfrac{x}{2^n}}
\end{align}$$

\text{arccsc}(\tfrac{1}{x}) & = \arcsin (x) \\

\end{align*}
