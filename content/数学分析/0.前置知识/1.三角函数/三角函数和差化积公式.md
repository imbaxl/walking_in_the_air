# 1. 和差化积公式



$$
\begin{align*} 
&\sin\alpha + \sin\beta=2\sin\dfrac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\cos\dfrac{\alpha-\beta}{2} \tag{1}\\
&\sin\alpha - \sin\beta=2\cos\dfrac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\sin\dfrac{\alpha-\beta}{2} \tag{2}\\
&\cos\alpha + \cos\beta=2\cos\dfrac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\cos\dfrac{\alpha-\beta}{2} \tag{3}\\
&\cos\alpha - \cos\beta=-2\sin\dfrac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\sin\dfrac{\alpha-\beta}{2} \tag{4}&
\end{align*}
$$
