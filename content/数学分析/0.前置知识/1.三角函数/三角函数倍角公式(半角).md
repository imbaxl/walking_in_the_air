# 1. 倍角公式



$$
\begin{align*} 
\sin 2x& = 2\sin x\cos x=\dfrac{2\tan x}{1+\tan^2x}  \\
\cos 2x& = \cos^2 x - \sin^2 x  \\
& = 2\cos^2 x -1 \\
& = 1-2\sin^2 x &\\
& = \dfrac{1-\tan^2x}{1+\tan^2x}\\
\tan 2x& = \dfrac{2\tan x}{1-\tan ^2x}
\end{align*}
$$




# 2. 半角公式


$$\begin{align}
&\sin^2 \dfrac{x}{2}=\dfrac{1-\cos x}{2}\\&\cos^2\dfrac{x}{2}=\dfrac{1+\cos x}{2}\\&\tan \dfrac{x}{2}=\dfrac{\sin x}{1+\cos x}=\dfrac{1-\cos x}{\sin x}
\end{align}$$
