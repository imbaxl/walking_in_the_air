# 1. 二角和差公式



$$
\begin{align*} 
\sin(\alpha \pm \beta) = \sin\alpha \cdot \cos\beta \pm \cos\alpha \cdot \sin\beta \tag{1}\\
\cos(\alpha + \beta) = \cos\alpha \cdot \cos\beta + \sin\alpha \cdot \sin\beta \tag{2}\\
\cos(\alpha - \beta) = \cos\alpha \cdot \cos\beta - \sin\alpha \cdot \sin\beta \tag{3}\\
\\\
\tan (\alpha+\beta) = \dfrac{\tan \alpha + \tan \beta}{1-\tan\alpha\tan\beta} \tag{4}\\
\tan (\alpha-\beta) = \dfrac{\tan \alpha - \tan \beta}{1+\tan\alpha\tan\beta} \tag{5}\\
\cot (\alpha+\beta) = \dfrac{\cot\alpha\cot\beta-1}{\cot \alpha + \cot \beta} \tag{6}\\
\cot (\alpha-\beta) = \dfrac{\cot\alpha\cot\beta+1}{\cot \alpha - \cot \beta} \tag{7}&
\end{align*}
$$
