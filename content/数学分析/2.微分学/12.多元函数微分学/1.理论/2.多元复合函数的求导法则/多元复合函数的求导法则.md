>[!thm] 定理1（链式法则，复合函数情形）
>设 $\boldsymbol{g}$ 在 $(u_0, v_0) \in D_g$ 点可导，即 $x = x(u, v)$，$y = y(u, v)$ 在 $(u_0, v_0)$ 点可偏导。记 $x_0 = x(u_0, v_0)$，$y_0 = y(u_0, v_0)$，如果 $f$ 在 $(x_0, y_0)$ 点<font color="#c00000">可微</font>，那么
>$$
>\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}(u_0, v_0) = \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}(x_0, y_0)\frac{\partial x}{\partial u}(u_0, v_0) + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}(x_0, y_0)\frac{\partial y}{\partial u}(u_0, v_0)
>$$
>$$
>\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}(u_0, v_0) = \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}(x_0, y_0)\frac{\partial x}{\partial v}(u_0, v_0) + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}(x_0, y_0)\frac{\partial y}{\partial v}(u_0, v_0)
>$$


>[!thm] 定理2（链式法则，向量值函数情形）
>设 $\boldsymbol{g}$ 在 $\boldsymbol{x}^0 \in D_g$ 点可导，即 $y_1, y_2, \cdots, y_m$ 在 $\boldsymbol{x}^0$ 点可偏导，且 $f$ 在 $\boldsymbol{y}^0 = \boldsymbol{g}(\boldsymbol{x}^0)$ 点可微，则
>$$
>\frac{\partial z}{\partial {x}_i}(\boldsymbol{x} ^0)= \frac{\partial z}{\partial y_1}(\boldsymbol{y^0})\frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_i}(\boldsymbol{x}^0) + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y_2}(\boldsymbol{y^0})\frac{\partial y_2}{\partial x_i}(\boldsymbol{x}^0) + \cdots + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y_m}(\boldsymbol{y}^0)\frac{\partial y_m}{\partial x_i}(\boldsymbol{x}^0),\ i = 1, 2, \cdots, n
>$$
>上式可以用矩阵表示为
>$$
>\left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial x_1}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial x_2}, \cdots, \frac{\partial z}{\partial x_n}\right)_{x = x^0} = \left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial y_1}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y_2}, \cdots, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y_m}\right)_{y = y^0}
\begin{pmatrix}
\tfrac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_1} & \tfrac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_2} & \cdots & \tfrac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_n} \\
\tfrac{\partial y_2}{\partial x_1} & \tfrac{\partial y_2}{\partial x_2} & \cdots & \tfrac{\partial y_2}{\partial x_n} \\
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
\tfrac{\partial y_m}{\partial x_1} & \tfrac{\partial y_m}{\partial x_2} & \cdots & \tfrac{\partial y_m}{\partial x_n}
\end{pmatrix}_{x = x^0}
>$$
>或用向量值函数的导数记号表为
>$$
>(f \circ g)'(x_0) = f'(y_0)g'(x_0).
>$$

<font color="#9c81ca">分析</font>
1. 此处的 $y_{i}$ 就是中间函数，从矩阵的运算来看就是，函数对所有末端变量 $x_{k}\ ( k=1,2,\cdots,n)$ 求偏导，那么就需要遍历所有中间函数 $y_{i}$，比如 $\dfrac{\partial{z}}{\partial{x_{1}}}$ 应当是所有的 $\sum\limits_{i=1}^{m}\dfrac{\partial{z}}{\partial{y_{i}}}\dfrac{\partial y_{i}{}}{\partial{x_{1}}}$，不断重复下去，就能还原出矩阵的形式.



>[!prp] 例题3.1
>设 $z = \arctan(xy)$，$y = e^x$，求 $\left.\dfrac{dz}{dx}\right|_{x=0}$.

<font color="#00b050">解</font>

1. 用矩阵语言表达：
$$
\begin{pmatrix} \dfrac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x} \end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix} \dfrac{\partial{z}}{\partial{x}} & \dfrac{\partial{z}}{\partial{y}} \end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix} \dfrac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}x}\\\dfrac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x} \end{pmatrix}
$$
2. 容易算出是 $1$.
